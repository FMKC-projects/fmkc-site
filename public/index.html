Get to know me

My name is Oliver Granlund and I go around the web with the username olligranlund (now you know my nickname). I’m a Finlandssvensk tech enthusiast located in the Helsinki region in Finland and I’m employed at Evermade as a senior developer. I also have my own company for side gigs like web development, photography and keyboard assemblies.

My spare time is spent for administrating the Finnish Mechanical Keyboard Community, tinkering (eg. soldering or brewing beer), playing videogames and doing sports. I often tend to get inspired by the most weird things and hence am ready to give anything a shot!
