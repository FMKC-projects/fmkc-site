Photography

One of my more expensive hobbies, doing photo sessions as semi-professional.

I started my photo & videography career in the nightclubs in Helsinki. I was capturing the mood of the evenings and making a shortfilm from every friday night.

As time went by, I started receiving non-nightclub photography requests, this included photoshoots, weddings, annual balls and other ceremonies. I still do photography but try to keep it as a side hobby.

I’ll update this page every once in a while, keep an eye for it ;)
